Overview

Meet the Galaxy A55 5G. The simple yet improved Galaxy A series comes in an iconic design featuring a 3-camera layout in a metal flat frame design for an easy and intuitive grip. Plus, it’s built with Corning® Gorilla® Glass Victus®+ making it tougher and more damage-resistant.

The Samsung Galaxy A55 5G offers impressive speed, reliability, and stunning features at an affordable price. It is designed for users who seek powerful performance, a vibrant display, and 5G connectivity in a sleek package.

The 5000mAh battery is built to last all day, and with fast charging capabilities, you'll spend more time using your phone and less time tethered to a charger.
