National Museum of the Philippines

A premier museum complex that includes the National Museum of Fine Arts, Anthropology, and Natural History, it houses works of national artists and significant artifacts from Philippine archaeology and natural history.

View

Ayala Museum

The museum offers dioramas of Philippine historical events, pre-colonial gold artifacts, and a remarkable collection of Filipino fine art. It also showcases modern and contemporary exhibits.

View

Bahay Tsinoy (The Filipino-Chinese Museum)

This museum highlights the contributions and integration of Chinese Filipinos throughout the country's history with exhibits about migration, commerce, and cultural exchange.

View

The Mind Museum

Known for its interactive science exhibits, it covers topics like biology, physics, and astronomy through hands-on installations that appeal to both children and adults.

View

Museo Pambata

A child-centric museum that focuses on experiential learning, it features exhibits on Filipino culture, environmentalism, health, and science, aimed at educating younger generations.
