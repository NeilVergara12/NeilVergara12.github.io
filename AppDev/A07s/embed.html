Coffee Recipe Book

Spanish Latte

A creamy mix of espresso and sweetened condensed milk topped with steamed milk.

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup steamed milk
Americano

Espresso diluted in hot water for a light, rich taste.

  • 1–2 shots of espresso
  • 3/4 cup hot water
Mocha

Espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup topped with whipped cream.

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1 tbsp chocolate syrup
  • 3/4 cup steamed milk
  • Whipped cream (optional)
Cappuccino

A balanced mix of espresso, steamed milk, and foamed milk.

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1/3 cup steamed milk
  • 1/3 cup milk foam
Latte

A creamy drink with more milk than a cappuccino and a little foam.

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 2/3 cup steamed milk
  • Light layer of milk foam
Flat White

Similar to a latte but with a higher ratio of coffee to milk and a velvety microfoam.

  • 1–2 shots of espresso
  • 1/2 cup steamed milk
Macchiato

A shot of espresso with just a dollop of steamed milk or foam.

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1 tbsp steamed milk or foam
Cold Brew

Coffee steeped in cold water for an extended time, served chilled.

  • 1 cup coarsely ground coffee
  • 4 cups cold water
  • Steep for 12–24 hours
Iced Coffee

Regular brewed coffee chilled and served over ice.

  • 1 cup brewed coffee
  • Ice cubes
  • Milk or sweetener (optional)
